Allison Rae Davis

JavaScript Developer & Word Nerd

Hi, I'm Allison.

I grew up in Anchorage, Alaska, but I've called Portland home for over eight years now. I have over nine years experience in arts administration and program development in San Francisco and Portland. In spring 2015, I began learning web development online. I instantly loved the satisfaction of understanding the architecture underneath the surface of websites and apps.

I'm also passionate about literary fiction, children's books, crossword puzzles and other word nerdery, digging in my garden, and growing vegetables. I skated for five seasons with the Rose City Rollers, Portland's flat-track roller derby league, and in 2015 captained my team to an undefeated season and a championship win.

I'm looking for work and I'm also looking to connect with folks in the Portland tech community and beyond who are making cool things and are passionate about learning.
